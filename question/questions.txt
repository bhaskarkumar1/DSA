Maximum Subarray Sum
Problem Statement :

Amy has an array A of N integers. In one operation she can increase an element of the array by K. She can do this operation at most M times.

She can also increase the same element multiple times. After performing these operations, she wants to know the maximum subarray sum that she can get.

If she does the operations optimally, what is the maximum subarray sum she can get.

We define subarray as continuous segment of the array with length>=0. NOTE: She has to maximise the maximum subarray sum.

Constraints :

1 <= N <= 2105 1 <= M <= 109 | Ai |, | K | <= 109 All input values are integers.

Input Format :

First-line contains three space separated integers N, M and K. Next line contains N space separated integers A1, A2, ...., AN.

Output Format :

Print a single integer denoting the maximum subarray sum that she can get after performing the operations optimally.

Sample Input :

5 2 2 -10 2 -1 5 0

Sample Output:

10

Explanation of Sample:

She would perform operation on the 3rd and 2nd index, So array becomes [ -10, 4,1,5,0] , Maximum Subarray sum is = 10 : [-10, 4, 1, 5, 0]
